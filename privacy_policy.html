Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Welcome to Skilli POS. This page outlines our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information, data structures, and permissions used inside our application.

🔒 Privacy Commitment: Skilli POS is a fully offline application. Your inventory catalogs, bill histories, tax summaries, and transactions are stored locally on your device. We do not host, upload, or transmit your business data to external servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better user experience while using our Service, the app functions purely offline. Any details recorded (such as items database, shop details, or transaction histories) are stored inside your device's local memory (via Hive database). No personal business statistics are shared with or uploaded by the developer.

However, the app utilizes third-party services that may collect information to identify you. You can review the policies of our third-party provider below:

  • Google Play Services

2. Permissions Requested & Usage

To enable offline POS billing features, barcode scanning, and thermal printing, the application requests the following device permissions:

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
Required to scan product barcodes using the physical phone camera.
Bluetooth Connect & Scan
Required to locate and connect to your Bluetooth thermal receipt printers to print generated invoices.
Storage Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)
Used to export sales records and product catalogs into backup Excel sheets, and to select optional shop logos from your gallery.
Vibration Control (android.permission.VIBRATE)
Used to trigger a short vibration feedback upon a successful barcode scan.

3. Security

We value your trust in selecting Skilli POS. Since all of your retail inventory and sales details are stored locally on your device, the ultimate safety of your data depends on your physical device security and local backup configurations (e.g., exporting Excel spreadsheets frequently). We suggest safeguarding your phone with a passcode or biometric screen locks.

4. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
